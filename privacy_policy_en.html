3.3 Anti-Fraud System for Tokens

‚ö†Ô∏è IMPORTANT - HOW TOKEN RESTORATION WORKS:

To prevent abuse and fraud, kAIboard implements an anti-fraud system whereby only ONE device at a time can use purchased tokens.

Behavior during restoration:

  1. Device A purchases 50,000 tokens ‚Üí Device A is "active" ‚úÖ
  2. Device B (same Google Play account) clicks "Restore purchases" ‚Üí Device B becomes "active" ‚úÖ
  3. Device A is automatically "invalidated" ‚Üí Tokens on Device A are zeroed ‚ùå
  4. To reactivate Device A: click "Restore purchases" again on Device A

Metadata saved for the anti-fraud system:

  • active_device: ID of the currently active device
  • invalidated_at: Timestamp when a device was deactivated
  • invalidated_reason: Reason for deactivation (e.g., "migrated_to_android_xxx")
  • migrated_from: Source device of the tokens
  • migration_timestamp: Date/time of migration

Legal basis: Legitimate interest to prevent fraud and abuse of the purchase system (Art. 6.1.f GDPR)

üí° NOTE: This system ensures that purchased tokens are not fraudulently duplicated across multiple devices. Token migration is irreversible: when you restore on Device B, Device A permanently loses the tokens until you perform a new restoration on Device A.

3.4 Purchase Verification

  • ‚úÖ Handle refunds and disputes with Google Play
